Modern Love Interest — Character Overview

Concept

The modern love interest is a contemporary woman who becomes Brunel’s guide, foil, challenger, and eventual partner.

She is not a replacement for Mary.  
She represents the future Brunel must choose.

---

Possible Roles

- Civil engineer  
- Transport planner  
- GIS specialist  
- Archaeologist  
- Infrastructure journalist  
- Environmental scientist  

---

Personality Traits

- Sharp  
- Grounded  
- Technically competent  
- Unimpressed by Brunel’s theatrics  
- Slowly realises he is exactly who he claims  

---

Narrative Beats

- Meets Brunel at the HS2 breach site  
- Initially assumes he is a reenactor  
- Becomes fascinated by his knowledge  
- Helps him navigate modern bureaucracy  
- Challenges his Victorian assumptions  
- Develops genuine affection  

---

Emotional Arc

She helps Brunel:

- accept the present  
- let go of the past  
- rebuild himself  
- reconnect emotionally  

Their relationship mirrors the restoration of the network.
